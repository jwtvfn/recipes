tea-recipe :)

Ingredients:
- 1 cup boiling water
- Tea Bag
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 slice lemon


Directions:
1. Boil the water
2. Put tea bag into a cup
3. Pour the water into the cup
4. Wait for 3-4 minutes
5. Take out the tea bag
6. Pour the sugar into the cup and stir
7. Put the lemon slice into the cup
